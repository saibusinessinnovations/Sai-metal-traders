Products we trade

Each category is prepared for locally stored product images, so final launch photos remain controlled and do not change randomly after deployment. Final acceptance depends on grade, recovery, contamination, quantity and inspection.

Aluminium

Aluminium Scrap

UBC cans, 6063 extrusion, aluminium wheels, cast aluminium, sheet aluminium and mixed aluminium loads.

Copper

Copper Scrap

Simple copper scrap categories including copper wire, birch/cliff copper, millberry copper and mixed copper lots.

Stainless Steel

Stainless Steel Scrap

Stainless steel 304, 316, 430 and mixed stainless industrial scrap subject to grade verification.

Brass

Brass Scrap

Yellow brass, red brass, 360 brass, brass turnings and sorted brass recyclable metal.

Nickel

Nickel and Alloy Scrap

Nickel scrap, Inconel, Monel, Hastelloy, superalloy turnings and high-temperature alloy material.

Ferrous

Steel and Ferrous Scrap

HMS 1 and 2, shredded steel, cast iron, structural steel and industrial ferrous scrap.

Lead

Lead and Battery Scrap

Lead scrap and battery-related recyclable material where legally acceptable and compliant with destination rules.

Mixed

Mixed Industrial Scrap

Motors, radiators, transformers, zinc, tin-bearing material, e-scrap and sorted industrial scrap streams.

Prepared Lots

Container Ready Material

Sorted, inspected and packed material prepared for truckload or export container movement.
